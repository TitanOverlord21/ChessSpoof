How to Play

  • Click a piece to highlight where it can move (yellow squares with square labels). Click a highlighted square to move there, or click anywhere else to cancel.
  • Rook: Moves in straight lines along its row or column. It may pass through empty squares but stops at the first piece it reaches, which it can capture.
  • Knight: Moves diagonally up to three squares in any of the four diagonal directions. Any enemy pieces along that diagonal path are captured.
  • Pawn: Jumps exactly two squares away in any horizontal, vertical, or diagonal direction (eight possible landing squares).
  • Bishop, Queen, and King: Cannot move yet.
  • When a piece lands on an enemy, that piece is captured and removed (knights also capture enemies along their travel path).
  • Each player’s score is the total value of their remaining pieces (Pawn 1, Bishop 2, Rook 5, Knight 8, King 12, Queen 15). You win when your opponent’s score drops to 5 or below.
