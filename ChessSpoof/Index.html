How to Play

  • Click a piece to highlight where it can move (yellow squares). Click a highlighted square to move there, or click anywhere else to cancel.
  • Rook: May move to any square on the same row or same column.
  • Knight: May move diagonally up to three squares in any of the four diagonal directions. Any enemy pieces along that diagonal path are captured.
  • Pawn, Bishop, Queen, and King: Cannot move yet.
  • Moving onto a square with an enemy piece captures it (rooks capture only the destination square).
  • Each player’s score is the total value of their remaining pieces. You win when your opponent’s score drops to 5 or below.
